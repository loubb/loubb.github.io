2. Winston Churchill Duologue

P1: The British Empire and the French Republic, linked together in their cause and in their need,
will defend to the death their native soil, aiding each other like good comrades to the utmost of their strength.

P2: Even though large tracts of Europe and many old and famous states have fallen or may fall into the grip of the Gestapo and all the odious apparatus of Nazi rule, we shall not flag or fail.

Chorus
We shall not flag or fail
We shall never surrender
We shall go on to the end
Whatever the cost may be

P1: We shall go on to the end, we shall fight in France, we shall fight on the seas and oceans, we shall
fight with growing confidence and growing strength in the air, we shall defend our island, whatever the cost
may be.

Chorus
We shall not flag or fail
We shall never surrender
We shall go on to the end
Whatever the cost may be

P2: We shall fight on the beaches, we shall fight on the landing grounds, we shall fight in the fields and in
the streets, we shall fight in the hills;

P1: We shall never surrender, and even if, which I do not for a moment believe, this island or a large part
of it were subjugated and starving, then our Empire beyond the seas, armed and guarded by the British fleet,
would carry on the struggle, until, in God's good time, the new world, with all its power and might, steps forth to the rescue and the liberation of the old."

Chorus
We shall not flag or fail
We shall never surrender
We shall go on to the end
Whatever the cost may be

Translation into Spanish

Nosotros no fallaremos.                we shall not fail          
Nunca nos rendiremos                   we shall never surrender   
Vamos hasta el final                   we shall go on to the end

3. Remote Host

HABIB: Lebanese-Australian Computer-Expert (from Melbourne)
JAYE: Habib's sister's Pen-Pal (from Brixton/London)

J: So, Amira said that you can show me how to get root on a remote box. Show me!

H: Jaye - listen to me carefully. This is a dangerous path. You have no idea about the forces at work here. My best friend from high-school once said to someone what you just said to me.

He was the captain of the football-team. He was the captain of the debating-team. All the boys wanted to be him. All the girls wanted to be with him.

Nowadays, he lives in a caravan in his parents' drive-way, and scrounges for cigarette-butts outside the local off-licence.

Don't go down this path, Jaye. It's not worth it! WORK IN PROGRESS
